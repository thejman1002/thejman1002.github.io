About 2ndStringers

Welcome to 2ndStringers, we are a fantasy football league dedicated to having fun and watching football. I (The Commish) have created this website to help and ease the flow of fantasy football. Here I will publish news, injuries, leaders, trading blocks, paying dues, and much more. As the commish I want to make this as fun and as easy as possible. So take a look around, let me know what you think and let the games begin!
